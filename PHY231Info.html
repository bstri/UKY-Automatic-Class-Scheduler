PHY 231

General University Physics4.0 Credits

Prereq or concur: MA 113.

Applies to the UK Core requirement: I I Natural/Physical/Mathmatical Science

Credit from this course applies to the following programs: Undergraduate

First part of a two-semester survey of classical physics. Consequences of the principles of mechanics are developed conceptually, analytically and quantitatively. Lecture, three hours; recitation, one hour per week.
